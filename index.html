What is League of Legends?

League of Legends is a MOBA (Mulitplayer Online Battle Arena) type game. The goal in League of Legends is to defeat the opposing team by destroying the enemy nexus. In a game, you are put into a team of five, and there are five possible roles you can choose to play: Toplane, Midlane, ADC, Support, or Jungle. Soon you will hopefully understand what these roles do, and who plays them. After learning all of the roles, and what they do, you will hopefully be able to choose the role that you want to play.
